Who We Are

CGC is an international logistics provider based in the New York & New Jersey area of the United States and Lagos, Nigeria. We offer air freight forwarding, ocean freight logistics, documentation, and customs clearance services to our clients for international and global operations covering major cities in Asia, Europe, and Africa.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted, reliable ground and airfreight forwarding company with efficiency and low cost; thus, making each shipment the most important thing we do.

Our Mission

To deliver the highest standards of product quality to support business logistics within the ground and air transportation industry and to achieve complete customer satisfaction through innovation and refinement of service levels.
