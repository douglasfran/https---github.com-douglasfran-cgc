Fraud Alert

Citizens Global Cargo is committed to protecting our customers from fraud. Please review the information below to stay informed and safeguard your sensitive information.

Citizens Fraud Alert

Citizens Global Cargo will use all reasonable and best efforts to prevent any fraudulent use of the Citizens Global Cargo service. Citizens Global Cargo LLC works diligently to prevent credit card fraud and is obliged to report any fraudulent or suspected fraudulent activity or any customer or prospective customer engaged in such activity and return the merchandise to the merchant.

Citizens Global Cargo LLC requires documentation from customers or prospective customers to prevent fraud, such as copies of government-issued photo identification (Driver's license, Green Card, work permit, or International passport). Citizens Global Cargo LLC also prohibits the use of its service for multi-level marketing, pyramid schemes, receiving checks and/or money orders, and other business practices easily subject to abuse. You may not use CITIZENS GLOBAL CARGO LLC address as your billing address.

Protect Against Fraud

At Citizens Global Cargo, we take fraud and the protection of our customers' information seriously. By providing extra layers of security for online shipping, we help you keep sensitive information safe. Please be aware that the following categories of fraud are the most commonly found:

  • Websites fraudulently using the Citizens Global Cargo's name or brand
  • Spam and phishing emails fraudulently using the Citizens Global Cargo name or brand
  • Phone calls, letters, faxes, and other communications fraudulently using the Citizens Global Cargo name or brand
  • Work From Home Scams

Tips to Protect Yourself

Being aware and protecting your sensitive information is the best way to prevent fraud. Here are some helpful tips:

  • Never respond to or act upon emails or select web links from unknown sources that require you to provide, update, or verify personal, financial, or other confidential information.
  • Be wary of any suspicious emails or phone calls asking for you to validate your credit or debit card account.
  • Use a secure internet browser and up-to-date virus protection software.
  • Do not give out your bank ID, ATM, or credit card password to anyone.
  • Change your password regularly and create strong passwords using a combination of letters and numbers.
  • Do not access confidential information at internet cafes, public libraries, etc.
  • Never click the 'save my password/details' option sometimes offered.

Fake Email Tell-Tale Signs

Be cautious of fake emails. Here are some signs to look out for:

  • Poor Grammar: Often, there are spelling or grammatical errors.
  • Wrong Address: The email is from an address that does not belong to the company in question. Emails from Citizens Global Cargo end with @citizensglobalcargo.com or .net.
  • Personal Information: The email asks for personal information, such as a password or account number. Unless there was a previous communication, we would not ask for this information via email.
